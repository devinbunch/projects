1.Briefly explain the policy variations induced by the NBP program. a. What does variable “nbp”= 1 indicate? b. What does variable “summer”= 1 indicate? c. What does variable “post = 1”indicate?

  1. The variable nbp is equal to 1 if the state is regulated by the NBP, and thus must take action against their NOx emission problem. Indicator—dummy variable.
  2. The variable summer is in reference to the season of the year. When “summer” is equal to 1, the data was collected during a time period wherein the season was currently Summer. Indicator—dummy variable.
  3. The variable post describes the time period after treatment started. Here, the treatment was administered in the year 2003, so our post variable serves as an indicator equal to 1 if the data observation was recorded after the cut-off time treatment started.
